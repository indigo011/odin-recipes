How to make a delicious baked rice

Description

For those moments that you need a practical and delicious meal, the baked rice is the right choice.

ingredients

  • Baked rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomatou sauce
  • vegetables
  • 2 sliced tomatos
  • Mozzarela cheese/li>
  • Oil
  • Kingdoms spice

Steps

  1. Mix the vegetables with the rice/li>
  2. Refoge the onion with the chicken and the tomatos
  3. Put salt and the spices
  4. Take the rice to the oven for 15 minutes

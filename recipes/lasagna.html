How to make a delicious lasagna

Description

My friend loves lasagna. Also, Girfield. Why not learn how to do this delicious italian food?

Just a few ingredients are requiered, so take a piece a paper and note it down.

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
